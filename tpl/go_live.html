project staffing

Our teams have worked with all major EMRs, including: Epic, McKesson, Cerner, AllScripts, eClinicalWorks, Practice Fusion, GE Healthcare, and many more

You’ve done a great job, built your team of IT and informatics specialists, started user training, and are now counting the weeks to a seamless go-live. But have you done enough to make sure your providers have the support they need? At Essia we believe that physician engagement cannot be underestimated and is the primary driver of go-live success.

Our team of specialists is adept at integrating with your staff to deliver proven physician engagement strategies that reduce the time from issue to resolution. By solving problems at-the-elbow, we free-up valuable IT resources to fix, rather than identify, issues. Our combination of clinical knowledge, customer service, and technical savvy will help you round out your go-live support plan.

project management

Our managers have seen it all. Once engaged, they conduct a site walkthrough to establish a comprehensive support plan using our proprietary Zone Support Model. By customizing support levels facility-wide throughout the day, our management staff makes certain that help is always close at hand. We carefully track physician workflows to follow providers through their day, and match support to departments, building capability and rapport with your providers over the duration of the go-live.

We pride ourselves on providing turn-key solutions to complex support engagements. Let us handle the logistics, so you can direct your attention where it’s needed most.

Your IT department has a valuable role to play in your go-live, and we recognize the importance of maximizing their time. Issue tracking & Management

Keeping track of all the moving pieces during a go-live can be difficult, so our onsite team monitors issues system wide, as they happen. When an issue arises, it’s important to understand it, then communicate resolutions quickly, so we keep our team well-informed with status updates and pertinent information in real time. This also helps us to adapt swiftly to new processes or workflows, all the while ensuring your users are fully informed on issue status and progress. Supporting our partners with advanced issue tracking is just one way we ensure prompt, consistent resolutions with minimal downtime.
